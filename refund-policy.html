Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

At Sri Sai Chicken Centre, we strive to provide the freshest products. Due to the perishable nature of our products (Fresh Chicken), we have specific policies regarding cancellations and refunds.

1. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your order under the following conditions:

  • Orders can be cancelled before they are processed or dispatched for delivery.
  • Once an order has been cut, packed, or dispatched, it cannot be cancelled.
  • To cancel an order, please call us immediately at +91 90324 46556 with your order details.

2. Refund Policy

Since we deal with fresh meat products, refunds are only applicable in the following scenarios:

  • Incorrect Item: If you received an item different from what you ordered.
  • Quality Issues: If the product is not fresh or has quality concerns at the time of delivery.
  • Non-Delivery: If the order was not delivered due to an error on our part.

Refunds will NOT be issued for:

  • Incorrect delivery address provided by the customer.
  • Customer not available to receive the order at the time of delivery.
  • Slight variations in weight (as meat cutting is a manual process).

3. Refund Process

If you are eligible for a refund:

  • Please report the issue within 1 hour of delivery.
  • Provide photos of the product and the receipt if requested.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method or as store credit, as per your preference.

4. Contact Us

For any queries related to cancellations or refunds, please reach out to us:

  • Phone: +91 90324 46556
  • Email: srisaichickencentre92@gmail.com
  • Address: Sri Sai Chicken Centre, Chilakuripet, Andhra Pradesh.
