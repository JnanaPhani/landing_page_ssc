Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Sri Sai Delivery Application and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

1. Information We Collect

While using Our Application, We may ask You to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify You. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Phone Number (for OTP verification)
  • Email address (for OTP or Google Sign-In)
  • First name and last name
  • Usage Data (Order history, app interactions)
  • Delivery Addresses and Landmark information

2. Use of Your Personal Data

The Company may use Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service: including to monitor the usage of our Service.
  • To manage Your Account: to manage Your registration as a user of the Service.
  • For the performance of a contract: the development, compliance and undertaking of the purchase contract for the products You have bought.
  • To contact You: To contact You by phone or email regarding updates or informative communications related to the orders, including security updates.
  • To manage Your requests: To attend and manage Your requests to Us.

3. Sharing Your Personal Data

We may share Your personal information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: We may share Your personal information with Service Providers (like delivery riders) to monitor and analyze the use of our Service and to deliver Your orders.
  • With Your consent: We may disclose Your personal information for any other purpose with Your consent.

4. Data Security

The security of Your Personal Data is important to Us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect Your Personal Data, We cannot guarantee its absolute security. We use industry-standard services like Supabase for secure data management.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to delete or request that We assist in deleting the Personal Data that We have collected about You. You may update, amend, or delete Your information at any time by signing in to Your Account, or by contacting us directly.
