Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Sri Sai Delivery is committed to delivering your fresh chicken orders in the shortest time possible. This policy outlines our delivery processes and conditions.

1. Delivery Areas

Currently, we provide delivery services exclusively within Chilakuripet, Andhra Pradesh. We do not ship products outside this local area as we deal with fresh, perishable meat.

2. Delivery Timeline

  • Standard Delivery: Most orders are delivered within 30 to 60 minutes from the time of order confirmation.
  • Pre-Orders: You can schedule deliveries for a specific time during our operating hours.
  • Delays: While we aim for prompt delivery, delays may occur due to high order volume, traffic, or weather conditions. We will notify you in case of significant delays.

3. Delivery Charges

Delivery charges are calculated based on the distance from our centre to your delivery location. The exact delivery fee will be shown in the app before you place your order.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you can track the real-time location of our delivery partner through the Sri Sai Delivery mobile application.

5. Delivery Protocol

  • Our delivery partner will call you upon arrival at the provided delivery address.
  • If you are unavailable, the partner will wait for a maximum of 10 minutes before the order is returned to the centre.
  • In case of a failed delivery due to customer unavailability, redelivery may incur additional charges.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your delivery, please contact us:

  • Address: Sri Sai Chicken Centre, Chilakuripet, Andhra Pradesh.
